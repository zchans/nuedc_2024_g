可靠性试验（ RELIABLILTY TEST ）
 1 High temp test
   After being placed in a chamber at 120℃ for 24 hours
 2 Low temp test
   After being placed in a chamber at -30℃ for 96 hours
 3 Humidity Test
   After being placed in a chamber +50℃ and 90±5% relative Humidity for 96 hours
 4 Vibration
   Sensor shall be measured after being applied vibration of Amplitude of 1.5mm with 10 to 55hz band
 of vibration frequency to each of 3 per-pendicuiar directions for 2 hours
 5 Drop test
  The part only shall be dropped from a height of 100cm onto a 10mm thick wooden board 3 times
 in 3 axes(X.Y.Z).(a total of 9 times).
 6 Treminal Mechanical strength
  The force 10 seconds of 9.8N(1.0Kg)is applied to each terminal in axial direction
  No damage and cutting off
7 After the test the part shall meet specifications with-out any Degradation in appearance and
performance except SPL after 4 hours at 25℃. The SPL should be in±10dB compared wit initial one.
